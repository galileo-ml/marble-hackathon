# Demo Script — "Step Into the Painting" (2 min)

## Opening (15 sec)

"What if you could walk into a painting? Not just look at it on a wall — but actually step inside and explore the world the artist imagined?

That's what we built. A 3D museum where you physically walk up to famous paintings and step inside them."

## The Problem / Inspiration (15 sec)

"Art museums are incredible — but you're always on the outside looking in. We wanted to use Marble's spatial AI to collapse that boundary. Take a 2D masterpiece, generate a full 3D world from it, and let you walk right in."

## Live Demo — Museum (30 sec)

*[Screen shows the museum. Walk around slowly with mouse.]*

"So here we are — this is our museum. This is itself a Marble-generated 3D world. You can see paintings on the walls — Vermeer, Van Gogh, Hopper, Matisse.

I'm going to walk up to Nighthawks by Edward Hopper..."

*[Walk toward Nighthawks, hint text appears]*

## Live Demo — Enter Painting (30 sec)

*[Walk into the painting — scene swaps instantly]*

"And just like that — we're inside the painting. This is a full 3D Gaussian splat world generated by Marble from Hopper's original artwork. I can look around, walk through the space, explore the diner from angles Hopper never painted."

*[Move around inside the world for a few seconds]*

## Live Demo — Return + Second Painting (15 sec)

*[Press X to return to museum]*

"Press X to return to the museum. Let me try one more — Bedroom in Arles by Van Gogh..."

*[Walk into Bedroom in Arles]*

"Now we're standing inside Van Gogh's bedroom. The colors, the perspective, the furniture — all reconstructed in 3D from a painting made in 1888."

## Technical + Wrap-up (15 sec)

"Under the hood — Marble generates 500k-point Gaussian splats from each painting. The museum itself is also a Marble world. We preload scenes as you approach so the transition is seamless — no loading screens, you just walk in.

Six paintings, six worlds you can explore. Thank you."

---

## Key Points to Hit (mapped to judging criteria)

**Spatial impact:** The entire experience is spatial — walking through a 3D museum, proximity-based triggers, physically entering paintings. Scale, movement, and proximity are the core mechanics.

**Innovation + Marble usage:** Marble is used twice — once to generate the museum environment, and again for each painting world. We're turning 2D art into explorable 3D spaces.

**Execution + demo quality:** The story is simple and universal — "walk into a painting." Live demo, no slides. The seamless transition is the wow moment.
