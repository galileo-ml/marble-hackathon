### Hackathon Overview

- 3.5 hour hackathon using marble technology
- Core constraint: must use marble in meaningful way
- Focus on experimentation over perfection
  - No expectation for polished slides or videos
  - Emphasis on exploration and trying new ideas

### Resources & Setup

- Starter kit contains:
  - World Lab documentation
  - WiFi password
  - Examples and quick start guides
  - Marvel coupon codes (everything free)
- Teams of 2-3 recommended (solo allowed, larger teams fine)
- Breakout rooms and outlets available in back/sides
- Food available outside room, bathrooms left of stage

### Timeline & Demos

- Hack until 8:30 PM
- Optional 2-minute lightning demos at 8:30
  - Sign-up sheet distributed at 8:30
  - Pre-generate world assets before demo
- Optional product demo for Harbor in 10 minutes

### Judging Criteria

- Judges: Yoko Lee (a16z partner), Diego Marcos (A-Frame co-creator), Ben (World Labs)
- Spatial impact: Does project fundamentally rely on 3D understanding
  - Focus on scale, movement, proximity
- Innovation and marble usage: Creative and effective implementation
- Execution and demo quality: Story/problem focus over polish
